Delivery pbf tiles from mbtiles

If your server run on your local and its port is 8836, your VT can be accessible at:
http://raspberrypi.local:8836/zxy/(mbtiles name)/{z}/{x}/{y}.pbf
*Try IP address if it does not work. (instead of raspberrypi, make sure that you use the name of your Raspbeery Pi.) e.g. http://rasberrypi400-123:8836/zxy/test/1/1/1.pbf
* Please confirm your mbtile is stored in mbtiles directory.

Delivery pbf tiles as static hosting

If your vector tiles are stored in "htdocs" directory, your VT can be accessible at:
http://raspberrypi.local:8836/(directory name in "htdocs")/{z}/{x}/{y}.pbf
